# Simple HR Suite

### The All-in-One Human Resources Management System

---

> **Manage your entire workforce from a single, intuitive platform.**
> From hiring to retirement, Simple HR Suite streamlines every aspect of human resources management — empowering your HR team, managers, and employees with the tools they need to succeed.

---

## Why Simple HR Suite?

Organizations today face growing complexity in managing their people. Scattered spreadsheets, manual attendance tracking, paper-based leave requests, and disconnected payroll processes lead to errors, delays, and frustrated employees.

**Simple HR Suite** replaces all of that with one unified platform that your entire organization can use — from the CEO reviewing performance dashboards to the employee clocking in from their phone.

## Core Modules

---

### Employee Management

Your employee database — centralized, organized, and always up to date.

- **Complete employee profiles** with personal details, job information, contact data, and emergency contacts
- **Photo management** with automatic avatar generation
- **Bilingual name support** for Arabic and English environments
- **Department organization** with color-coded grouping and manager hierarchy
- **Employee lifecycle tracking** from onboarding to exit
- **Bulk import/export** via CSV for easy data migration
- **WordPress user sync** — automatically create and link user accounts
- **QR code identity cards** — generate unique QR codes for each employee

---

### Attendance & Time Tracking

Accurate, automated attendance — no more manual timesheets.

- **Multiple clock-in methods:**
  - Self-service web widget (from any browser)
  - Kiosk terminals with QR code scanning
  - Manual entry by managers
- **Smart shift management** with per-day schedules and flexible configurations
- **Break tracking** — clock in, take breaks, clock out, all tracked automatically
- **Selfie verification** — optional photo capture during clock-in for identity confirmation
- **Geofence enforcement** — ensure employees are at the right location when they clock in
- **Configurable attendance periods** — align reports with your payroll cycle, not just the calendar month
- **Automatic status detection** — the system identifies late arrivals, early departures, absences, and incomplete shifts
- **Early leave management** — employees can request early departure with manager approval workflow

**Kiosk Mode** — Set up dedicated attendance terminals:
- Full-screen interface optimized for shared devices
- QR code scanner for fast employee identification
- Camera integration for selfie capture
- Offline queueing — punches are stored locally and synced when connectivity returns
- Geo-locked to specific locations

---

### Leave Management

Streamlined leave requests with multi-level approvals and automatic balance tracking.

- **Customizable leave types** — annual leave, sick leave, maternity, paternity, and any custom types you need
- **Automatic balance calculation** based on hire date and tenure
- **Tenure-based adjustments** — leave entitlements automatically increase with years of service
- **Gender-specific leave types** — configure leave for specific groups (e.g., maternity leave)
- **Multi-level approval workflow** — department manager and HR review
- **Leave cancellation and early return** — full support for changing plans
- **Holiday calendar** — define company holidays with optional yearly recurrence
- **Attachment support** — require documents like medical certificates for specific leave types
- **Real-time balance display** — employees always see their available leave at a glance

---

### Payroll Processing

From salary configuration to payslip generation — payroll made simple.

- **Flexible salary components** — define earnings, deductions, and benefits with fixed amounts, percentages, or custom formulas
- **Employee-level overrides** — customize salary components per employee when needed
- **Automated payroll calculation** integrating:
  - Attendance data (absence and late deductions)
  - Overtime calculations
  - Loan installment deductions
  - Government contributions (GOSI)
- **Payroll workflow** — Draft, Review, Approve, and Pay with full control at each stage
- **Payslip generation** — professional payslips available for employee self-service download
- **Bank integration** — IBAN tracking and bank transfer file generation
- **Configurable payroll periods** — monthly, bi-weekly, or weekly cycles

---

### Loans & Cash Advances

Complete loan lifecycle management with built-in approval chains.

- **Employee self-service requests** — submit cash advance requests directly from the portal
- **Two-level approval** — General Manager approval followed by Finance approval
- **Conditional approval** — approve a different amount than originally requested
- **Automatic installment scheduling** — monthly payment plans generated automatically
- **Payroll integration** — loan repayments deducted directly from salary
- **Payment tracking** — record payments, skip installments, and track outstanding balances
- **Full audit trail** — every approval, rejection, and payment is logged

---

### Performance Management

Measure, track, and improve employee performance with data-driven insights.

- **Attendance commitment scoring** — automatic performance scores based on attendance records
- **Goals & OKR tracking** — set objectives, track progress, and review achievement
- **Multi-reviewer system** — self-reviews, manager reviews, and peer reviews
- **Weighted performance scoring:**
  - 40% Attendance commitment
  - 35% Goals achievement
  - 25% Review ratings
- **Performance alerts** — automatic notifications when performance drops below thresholds
- **Department rankings** — compare performance across teams
- **Monthly snapshots** — automatic performance data capture for trend analysis
- **Visual dashboards** — charts and graphs for at-a-glance performance insights
- **Period comparison** — compare this month vs. last month performance side by side

---

### Hiring & Recruitment

Track candidates from application to hire with a structured approval pipeline.

- **8-stage candidate workflow:**
  1. Applied
  2. Screening
  3. HR Reviewed
  4. Department Pending
  5. Department Approved
  6. GM Pending
  7. GM Approved
  8. Hired / Rejected
- **Document management** — store resumes and cover letters with each candidate
- **Multi-level approval tracking** — notes and decisions documented at every stage
- **Automatic employee creation** — when a candidate is hired, their employee profile and user account are created instantly
- **Trainee program management** — track trainees with training periods, supervisors, university information, and automatic conversion to full employees

---

### Employee Exit & Settlement

Handle resignations and final settlements with structured workflows.

**Resignation Management:**
- Employee self-service resignation submission
- Multi-level approval chain (Manager, HR, Finance)
- Notice period tracking
- Handover documentation
- Automatic expiry for unprocessed resignations

**Final Settlement:**
- End-of-service gratuity calculation based on tenure
- Unused leave encashment
- Final salary proration
- Allowance and deduction adjustments
- Multi-step settlement approval workflow

**For International Workforces:**
- Government exit reference tracking
- Exit date management (expected and actual)
- Travel arrangement coordination
- Compliance documentation

### Asset Management

Track company assets from purchase to return with full accountability.

- **Complete asset inventory** — track every asset with code, serial number, model, purchase details, and warranty
- **QR code tagging** — auto-generated QR codes for every asset
- **Assignment workflow** — assign assets to employees with required acceptance
- **Photo documentation** — capture selfie and asset photo during assignment and return
- **Return workflow** — structured return process with manager initiation and condition tracking
- **Asset condition tracking** — New, Good, Damaged, Needs Repair, Lost
- **Invoice and warranty management** — store purchase documents and track warranty expiry
- **Department-level tracking** — see all assets assigned to each department

- **Configurable document types** — define required documents (ID copies, contracts, certifications, etc.)
- **Expiry tracking and alerts** — automatic notifications before documents expire
- **Update request workflow** — HR can request document updates, employees fulfill from their portal
- **Employee self-service upload** — employees upload and manage their own documents
- **Status indicators** — at-a-glance view of valid, expiring, and expired documents

### Shift Swap

Let employees swap shifts with structured approval workflows.

- **Employee-initiated swaps** — request to swap shifts with a colleague
- **Two-step approval:**
  1. Target employee accepts or declines the swap
  2. Manager reviews and approves or rejects
- **Automatic notifications** — all parties are notified at every step
- **Request tracking** — unique reference numbers (SS-YYYY-NNNN) for every swap request

## Works Anywhere

### Mobile-Ready Design
Every page in Simple HR Suite is optimized for mobile devices. Employees can clock in, request leave, or check their payslip from their phone — no app download required.

### Install as an App
Simple HR Suite can be installed as a Progressive Web App (PWA) on any smartphone. Tap "Add to Home Screen" and access your HR portal like a native app — with an icon on your home screen and a full-screen experience.

### Offline Capability
In areas with unreliable internet, the kiosk and attendance features continue to work offline. Punches are stored locally and automatically synced when connectivity is restored. Your operations never stop.
